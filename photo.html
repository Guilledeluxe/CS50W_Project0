My favourite Photographers

When starting out as a photographer, you may find yourself in a creative rut. A lack of ideas, or rather, an abundance of ideas can make it difficult to find a photographic theme.This is where the masters of photography can help you. This is a shortlist of some of the most famous photographers this world has ever seen.
Some of the photographers in our list have shifted from our mortal plane. Yet, their images, messages and creativity live on. We can all take something away from their careers and work.
  • Chema Madoz
  • Elliot Herwitt
  • Sebastiao Salgado
  • André Kertész
  • Diane Arbus
  • Annie Leibovit
  • Robert Frank
  • Peter Lindbergh
